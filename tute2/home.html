Southlands College - Galle
1885-2021

Southlands College is located inside one of the prestigious heritages in Sri Lanka; Galle Fort in Southern province. The school was started in 1885 by Methodist mission with a group of 55 students in a house in Galle Fort. The first principal of the school was Ms. Lucy Vandestratan. It is a girls school with over 2000 students and 250 teachers. There are over 10 buildings, 6 science laboratories, 3 computer laboratories, an auditorium, a main hall and many more.

Today Southlands College is shining bright with the guidane of the current principal Mrs. Shanthi Seneviratne. Over the past 132 years the past principals, past and present teachers put an enormous effort and helped all the girls achieve their dreams in their life.
