OUR VISION
Our vision is to transmit our college to the most prominent seat of learning in Sri Lanka,
begetting self-disciplined, morally-uplifted and perfectly skilled women,
knitted in love and service capable of rendering towards the well-being and betterment of the society.


OUR MISSION
Our mission is the task of mastering competences needed to create a conscientious woman perfected in mind,
full of love, disciplined in head, mind and body, immaculate in character and conduct capable of being accountable
and responsible for the sustainable development of Sri Lanka.
